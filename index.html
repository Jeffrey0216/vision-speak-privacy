Privacy Policy for Vision Speak

Vision Speak uses the device camera to capture images and analyze them using on-device machine learning for accessibility and scene understanding.

Data Collection

Vision Speak does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All image analysis is performed locally on the user’s device.

Permissions Used

  • Camera: Required to capture images for analysis.
  • Hardware Buttons: The device volume button is used as a shortcut to capture images for analysis.

Third-Party Services

Vision Speak uses Google ML Kit for on-device image analysis. No data is sent to external servers.
